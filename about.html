Our story — thoughtful hair, trusted hands

Temi Hair Salon began as a small atelier with a single chair and a big belief: hair is personal, and service should feel like hospitality. Over the years we've grown into a team of specialists who care about technique, hair health and the confidence our work gives clients.

How we craft

Every appointment starts with a consult: understanding hair history, lifestyle and desired outcomes. We choose formulas and techniques that prioritise hair integrity — from Olaplex repairs to bespoke balayage hand-painted for each client.

Community & education

We run monthly masterclasses, pro training and community styling days to keep our team sharp and give back to local stylists.

Sustainability

We prioritise ethically sourced products, refill programs and reduced-waste packaging. Small changes, consistent impact.

FAQs

What should I bring to my appointment? +
Reference photos, a list of recent products used and any allergy info. If you have extensions, bring your existing pieces for matching.
Do you offer consultations? +
Yes — consultations are complimentary and recommended for color transformations.
Are walk-ins accepted? +
We accept a limited number of walk-ins depending on availability. Booking ahead is recommended.

Our journey

A short timeline of the milestones that shaped Temi Hair Salon.
  1. 2016 — Temi opens a single-chair atelier focused on bespoke color work.
  2. 2018 — Team grows to 4 stylists; first training nights begin.
  3. 2020 — Expanded to Victoria Island studio; launched refill program.
  4. 2022 — Launched extensions service and community masterclasses.
  5. 2024 — Recognised for sustainability and launched multi-location pop-ups.
