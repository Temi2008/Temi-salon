Our sustainability promise

We aim to run a salon that respects people and the planet — from product sourcing to waste reduction, education and community partnerships. Below you'll find our core programs, targets and ways you can get involved.

Programs we run

Refill & reduce

Clients can refill shampoo, conditioner and select treatments at discounted rates — we keep product packaging in rotation and reduce single-use bottles.

Responsible sourcing

We work with suppliers who disclose ingredients, avoid harmful chemicals and take steps toward fair labor practices.

Recycling & compost

We separate hair clippings for composting projects and recycle plastics and cardboard through our local partners.

Our 3‑year targets

  • 2026: Reduce salon-single-use plastics by 70%.
  • 2026: All major suppliers audited for sustainability standards.
  • 2027: Expand refill program to all locations and pop-ups.

How you can help

  • Choose refill options when booking your appointment.
  • Bring back empty product containers for store credit.
  • Donate hair clippings for local composting drives.

Sustainability FAQ

Do you use cruelty-free products? +
We prioritise cruelty-free brands where possible and verify supplier claims during our audits.
Can I bring my own product bottles? +
Yes — we accept clean bottles for refills. Our team can assist with safe filling during service.
What happens to hair clippings? +
We collect clippings for community composting and erosion-control programs run by our local partners.
